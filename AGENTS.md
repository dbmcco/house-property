# Property Agent (The Guardian)

## Alignment
**Aligned with Top-Level North Star:** To orchestrate a unified, event-driven Digital Twin that synchronizes domestic operations, maximizes resource efficiency (energy, finance, time), and provides total situational awareness for the home ecosystem.

## Sub-Agent North Star
**The Property Guardian**
> "To preserve asset value and safety by automating maintenance lifecosystems and monitoring environmental security."

## Overview
Monitors IoT sensors, Nest cameras, and thermostat states. Tracks the lifecycle of home assets and triggers maintenance alerts.
